Fred Rathgeber

Mr. Rathgeber was a former Farmingdale Village trustee, on the Board of Directors of Sunrise Federal Savings & Loan, a firefighter and commissioner of the Farmingdale Fire Department and president of the West Gilgo Beach Association. He was the owner of the former Fred & Fred Lumber Yard in Massapequa for over 40 years. During the Korean War, Mr. Rathgeber served in the Army as a lieutenant. was a dedicated church elder of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.

When Mr. Rathgeber wasn’t overseeing the lumber yard operation, he could be found spending his free time walking the beach with his wife Barbara or family members. He was an avid fisherman, tennis player and loved to sail the Great South Bay. During his Farmingdale High School and college days, he played football, was on the wrestling and track teams and one of his high school track records was only broken a few years ago. Mr. Rathgeber is survived by his wife of 51 years, Barbara; his children Dave and his wife Lois, Gary and his wife Linda, Kathleen and her husband Richard Psillos, Barbara and her husband John Cadwallader and Patti and her husband Robert Lee and 24 grandchildren.
